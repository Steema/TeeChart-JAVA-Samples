Calculating and charting the Upper and Lower limits of an SPC Quality Control series.

This example includes formulae to calculate the SPC upper and lower limits. The Chart displays 2 series, one with the number of "good" parts and another with the percent of "bad" parts. With these two Series, the example calculates two values: the upper and lower limits.

These limits are displayed using another two series.

Correctness of calculation depends very much on your particular country's rules.
